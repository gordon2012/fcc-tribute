Thor, Son of Odin

  • 964: Thor is born.
  • 965: Loki is adopted by Thor's father, Odin.
  • 973: Loki turns into a snake and tricks Thor, stabbing him.
  • 2009: Thor defeats Imir, who kidnaps some dwarves.
  • 2010:
    • Thor is tricked by Loki, is banished to Earth, and meets Jane Foster
    • Thor proves himself worthy by defeating the Destroyer
    • Thor is forced to destroy the Bifrost Bridge, stranding Loki in the Sanctuary and himself in Asgard.
  • 2012:
    • Thor joins the Avengers to stop Loki's latest plot
    • Thor helps the Avengers stop the Chitauri attack on New York
    • Thor returns to Asgard with Loki and the Tesseract
  • 2013:
    • Thor defeats Malekith and his Dark Elves and obtains the Aether
    • Thor entrusts the Aethor to the Collector on planet Knowhere
  • 2015:
    • Thor helps the Avengers retrieve Loki's scepter from a HYDRA research facility
    • Thor helps the Avengers prevent the destruction of the Earth from the rogue super AI Ultron
  • 2017:
    • Thor defeats Surtur and retrieves his crown
    • Thor witnesses the death of Odin and the release of Hela
    • Thor is transported to the Grandmaster's planet and enslaved
    • Thor battles the Hulk in the arena
    • Thor and the Revengers travel through The Devil's Anus to Asgard
    • Thor uses the Eternal Flame on Sutur's crown to trigger Ragnarok, which destroys Asgard and Hela
  • 2018 (SPOILER ALERT):
    • Thor's ship is attacked by Thanos
    • Thor is rescued by the Guardians of the Galaxy
    • Thor forges the axe Stormbreaker from the heart of a dying star
    • Thor buries Stormbreaker into Thanos' chest
